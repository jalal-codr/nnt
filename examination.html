OUR EXAMINATIONS

School Based Examination

~Screening test - For primary six pupils seeking admission into Junior Secondary Schools with boarding facilities.

~ CAS/Placement test Oversees the conduct of Continous Assessment Scores/Placement Tests for Pupils in Primary Six across all Public and Approved Private Primary Schools and subsequent issuance of Uniform Attestation and Completion of Primary Six Document for participating pupils that may or may not likely transit to complete minimum education attainable by every Nigerian Child.

~ Basic Education Certificate Examination - Oversees the conduct of Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) for Students completing Junior Secondary School III in conclusion of Universal Baic Education.

Non-School Based Examination

~Combined Confirmation/Promotion Examination - for Clerical Officers and Assistants on GL 04/03 for the purpose of the Confirmation of their Appointment and Advancement to the next Grade Level.

~ Compulsory Examination - for newly employed Executive/Administrative Officers in the Public Service.

~ External Secretarial Examination For Confidential Secretaries and Typists Converting to Confidential Secretaries in the Public Service.

~ Trade Test -For Artisans in the Public Service and self- employed desirous of acquiring certificate of proficiency in their chosen profession to boost their status.
